Frequently Asked Questions

What is ColorPeek?

ColorPeek is a free web-based tool that helps you extract dominant colors from any image and generate beautiful, ready-to-use color palettes with HEX, RGB, and HSL values.

Who is ColorPeek for?

ColorPeek is perfect for UI/UX designers, graphic designers, frontend developers, digital artists, and anyone who works with color regularly.

How do I use ColorPeek?

Simply upload an image or paste an image URL. ColorPeek will analyze the image and present a full color breakdown, including palette options and contrast checking.

Can I use ColorPeek on mobile?

Yes! ColorPeek is fully responsive and works beautifully on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Is ColorPeek free to use?

Yes. ColorPeek is completely free with no login required. We may show ads in the future to support the service.

How accurate are the extracted colors?

ColorPeek uses pixel clustering and dominant color algorithms to provide high-accuracy color data from any image.

Will my uploaded images be stored?

No, your images are processed in-browser or temporarily in memory. We do not store any files or personal data.

We hope ColorPeek makes your color workflow easier and more fun. If you have feedback or want to suggest a feature, drop us a message through our contact page!
