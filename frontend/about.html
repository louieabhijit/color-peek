About ColorPeek

Your Go-To Color Palette Generator

Welcome to ColorPeek! We're a free, fast, and intuitive tool designed to help you extract dominant colors from any image. Whether you're a UI/UX designer, developer, artist, or digital creator, ColorPeek simplifies the way you create, manage, and work with color palettes.

Our Mission

Color is a core element of any great design. Our mission is to empower creatives by making it effortless to discover and use beautiful, harmonious color combinations sourced directly from your images.

Why We Built ColorPeek

We noticed a common challenge among designers and developers: jumping between tools just to grab hex codes or test color contrast. ColorPeek was created to centralize all these features into a single lightweight, browser-based tool. No installs, no bloat.

What Makes ColorPeek Unique?

One-Click Color Extraction

Upload your image and instantly get HEX, RGB, and HSL values.

Auto-Generated Palettes

Discover ready-to-use complementary, analogous, triadic, and monochrome color schemes.

Contrast Checker

Make sure your color combinations are accessible and visually effective.

Light & Dark Theme

Tailor your experience for day or night workflows.

Mobile-Friendly & Lightning Fast

Works seamlessly on any device, anywhere.

ColorPeek was built with love for designers who want powerful tools without the fluff. We're constantly improving it based on user feedback.
