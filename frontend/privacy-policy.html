Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2023

Introduction

At ColorPeek, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

ColorPeek is designed to operate without collecting personal data. We collect:

  • Images you upload for color extraction (these are processed on your device and not stored on our servers)
  • Anonymous usage data through analytics to improve our service
  • Browser cookies for theme preferences (dark/light mode)

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our service
  • Understand how our service is used
  • Develop new features and functionality

Cookies

We use cookies to store your theme preferences (dark/light mode). You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Third-Party Services

ColorPeek uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include:

  • Google Analytics (for usage statistics)
  • CDN providers for serving content

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. Images you upload for color extraction are processed in your browser and are not stored on our servers. Our service is designed to be privacy-focused.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@colorpeek.app
